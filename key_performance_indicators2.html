Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
  Target Measurement Performanace Targets
1. Participant Knowledge Improvement Achieve a 90% knowledge improvement score among participants as measured by pre- and post-game assessments. Pre- and post-game quizzes, participant feedback surveys. Knowledge Improvement: Achieve a minimum 90% increase in asset management knowledge.
2. Engagement and Participation Rate Maintain an engagement and participation rate of at least 95% throughout the game sessions. Attendance records, active participation tracking, engagement surveys. Engagement Rate: Maintain at least a 95% participation rate.
3. Compliance Understanding Ensure 100% of participants demonstrate a thorough understanding of compliance requirements as per ISO 55000 and GFMAM guidelines. Compliance-related quizzes, scenario-based assessments, participant feedback. Compliance Understanding: Ensure 100% compliance understanding.
4. Decision-Making Accuracy Achieve an 85% accuracy rate in decision-making scenarios based on real-world asset management situations. Scenario outcomes, decision-making assessment scores. Decision-Making Accuracy: Attain an 85% accuracy rate in decision-making scenarios.
5. Cost Reduction Strategies Identify and implement cost reduction strategies that result in a 10% decrease in simulated maintenance and operational costs. Cost savings analysis, participant strategy evaluations. Cost Reduction: Realize a 10% reduction in simulated maintenance costs.
6. Risk Management Proficiency Ensure 90% of participants can accurately assess and manage risks using the 5x5 risk matrix. Risk assessment exercises, scenario-based evaluations. Risk Management Proficiency: Ensure a 90% proficiency rate in risk assessment.
7. Asset Lifecycle Planning Achieve a 95% proficiency rate in lifecycle planning and management of assets. Lifecycle management exercises, participant evaluations, scenario outcomes. Lifecycle Planning Proficiency: Achieve a 95% proficiency in asset lifecycle planning.
8. Return on Net Assets (RONA) Improve the simulated RONA by at least 15% through strategic asset management decisions. Financial performance tracking, RONA calculations before and after game scenarios. RONA Improvement: Increase simulated RONA by at least 15%.
9. Participant Satisfaction Achieve a participant satisfaction rate of at least 90% as measured by post-game surveys. Participant feedback surveys, overall satisfaction ratings. Participant Satisfaction: Attain a 90% satisfaction rate.
10. Innovation and Continuous Improvement Encourage 100% of participants to propose at least one innovative idea or improvement based on their game experience. Idea submission records, participant feedback, implementation of viable ideas. Innovation Proposals: Encourage 100% participation in proposing innovative ideas.
By setting these performance targets and KPIs, we aim to ensure that the Asset Mastery Game not only provides a robust educational experience but also drives tangible improvements in asset management practices
