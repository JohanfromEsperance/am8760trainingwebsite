Utilities include essential services such as water supply, sewage systems, and waste management, supporting the overall functionality and environmental compliance of the port.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the management of utilities at the Port of Exsto is a complex, high-stakes operation integral to the port's overall functionality and success. By understanding the who, what, when, where, why, and how of these activities, stakeholders can better appreciate the intricacies involved and the importance of managing associated risks. Through advanced equipment, skilled personnel, and robust management practices, the Port of Exsto continues to excel in its utilities operations, ensuring efficiency, safety, and reliability in serving the global supply chain
