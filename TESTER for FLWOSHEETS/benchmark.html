Benchmark Details

Who: Asset managers, data analysts

What: Process of comparing asset performance and practices against industry standards

When: During strategic planning and periodic reviews

Where: Asset management and planning departments

With: Benchmarking tools, industry reports

How: By collecting data, analyzing performance, and comparing against benchmarks

Why: To identify areas for improvement and ensure competitive performance

Quality Check:

  • Right person (managers, analysts)
  • Right time (planning, reviews)
  • Right place (management, planning departments)
  • Right equipment (tools, reports)
  • Right process (collecting, analyzing, comparing)
  • Right work (improvement identification)
  • Right reason (competitive performance)

Expanded Activities:

  • Collect performance data for port assets.
  • Analyze data to identify trends, strengths, and weaknesses.
  • Compare data against industry benchmarks to identify areas for improvement.
  • Document findings and develop plans to implement improvements.

Research and Benchmarking:

  • Best Practices: Follow guidelines from the Benchmarking Exchange (TBEx) for asset benchmarking.
  • Case Studies: Study benchmarking reports from other ports to identify effective practices.
  • Benchmark Data: Use industry reports to compare your performance metrics with those of other ports.

Narrative: This block involves benchmarking the performance and practices of port assets against industry standards. It includes collecting performance data, analyzing it, and comparing it against benchmarks to identify areas for improvement and ensure that the port's asset management practices are competitive.
