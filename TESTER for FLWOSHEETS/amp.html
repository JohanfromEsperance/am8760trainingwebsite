AMP (Asset Management Plan) Details

Who: Asset managers, maintenance planners

What: Development of a detailed plan for managing port assets

When: During strategic planning and periodic reviews

Where: Asset management and planning departments

With: CMMS, asset data

How: By creating and implementing a detailed asset management plan

Why: To ensure effective management and maintenance of port assets

Quality Check:

  • Right person (managers, planners)
  • Right time (planning, reviews)
  • Right place (management, planning departments)
  • Right equipment (CMMS, data)
  • Right process (creating, implementing)
  • Right work (detailed plan)
  • Right reason (effective management)

Expanded Activities:

  • Develop a detailed plan for managing and maintaining port assets.
  • Use CMMS to track asset data and maintenance activities.
  • Implement the plan and monitor progress regularly.
  • Review and update the plan based on performance data and changing needs.

Research and Benchmarking:

  • Best Practices: Follow guidelines from the Institute of Asset Management (IAM) for asset management planning.
  • Case Studies: Review asset management plans from other ports to identify effective strategies.
  • Benchmark Data: Compare your asset management plan with industry standards to ensure alignment with best practices.

Narrative: This block involves developing and implementing an Asset Management Plan (AMP) to ensure the effective management and maintenance of port assets. This includes creating a detailed plan, using CMMS to track asset data, and regularly reviewing and updating the plan based on performance data and changing needs.
