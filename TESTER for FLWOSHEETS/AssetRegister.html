Asset Register

Definition: A comprehensive record of all assets, including details on ownership, location, condition, and remaining life.

Expanded Description: An Asset Register is a detailed and comprehensive record that lists all assets owned by an organization. It includes essential information such as asset ID, location, condition, and remaining useful life. The asset register serves as a central database for asset management, enabling organizations to track and manage their assets effectively. Regular updates and audits ensure the accuracy and completeness of the asset register.

Key Components:

  • Asset identification and description
  • Location and ownership details
  • Condition assessment and maintenance history
  • Remaining useful life and lifecycle information
  • Regular updates and audits
