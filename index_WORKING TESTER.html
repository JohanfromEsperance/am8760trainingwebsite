Welcome to Asset Mastery Plus

Welcome to AM8760, a cutting-edge training program designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of asset management principles and practices. This program is rooted in the guidelines and standards set by ISO 55001 and the GFMAM (Global Forum on Maintenance and Asset Management) Landscape Edition 3. AM8760 focuses on managing assets over the full 8760 hours in a year, emphasizing the importance of monetizing time at unit rates to express efficiency.

The AM8760 training program aims to equip asset managers, maintenance professionals, and organizational leaders with the skills and knowledge necessary to optimize asset value, ensure compliance, make informed investment decisions, manage human resources effectively, and minimize downtime. By the end of this program, you will be well-versed in the critical aspects of asset management and prepared to implement best practices within your organization.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Asset Value Management
  • Compliance
  • Investment
  • HR Management
  • Downtime Management

Alignment with ISO 55001 and GFMAM:

AM8760 is meticulously designed to align with ISO 55001 and the GFMAM Landscape Edition 3. This alignment ensures that the training program adheres to international best practices and standards in asset management, providing you with a solid foundation to build upon.

Training Outcomes:

  • Develop a strategic approach to asset management.
  • Improve your ability to manage risks and ensure asset reliability.
  • Enhance your skills in financial planning and investment decision-making.
  • Foster a culture of continuous improvement and compliance within your organization.
  • Optimize the efficiency and effectiveness of your asset management practices.

We encourage you to engage actively with the content, participate in discussions, and apply the concepts learned to real-world scenarios. Welcome to the journey of mastering asset management with AM8760!
