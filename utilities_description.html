Overview

17.0 Utilities

Overview

Utilities include essential services such as water supply, sewage systems, and waste management, supporting the overall functionality and environmental compliance of the port.

Key Components and Functions

  • Water Supply Systems
  • Sewage and Wastewater Treatment
  • Waste Management Systems

Life Cycle Costing and Maintenance

  • Preventive Maintenance
  • Corrective Maintenance
  • Condition-Based Maintenance
  • Lifecycle Cost Analysis

Criticality Levels and Risk Management

  • High Criticality: Water supply systems, sewage and wastewater treatment.
  • Medium Criticality: Waste management systems.
  • Low Criticality

Sustainability and Compliance

  • Environmental Compliance
  • Sustainability Initiatives
  • Health and Safety

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

  • Availability: Target 95%
  • Utilization: Target 85%
  • Maintenance Costs
  • Operational Efficiency

Conclusion

Utilities are vital to the operations at the Port of EXSTO. Effective management of these assets through regular maintenance, risk management, and sustainability practices ensures operational efficiency, compliance, and long-term value. By understanding the criticality levels and lifecycle costs, the port can make informed decisions to optimize the performance and reliability of its utility assets.
